About

Background

I'm a communicator who likes to make things — whether that's articles, videos, apps, bagels, podcasts, or stop-motion films.

This site has existed in some form since 1996, when it was danssmileyplace.com — dedicated to the research and development of emoticon language on a Gateway 2000. The major work of that era was a "full screen smiley" depicting Tom Cruise rappelling from the ceiling in Mission Impossible.

I studied Business and Political Science at the University of Michigan (Phi Beta Kappa), spent a summer on a trail crew in the Oregon wilderness, and have volunteered with 826 literacy programs in every city I've lived.

Based in Ann Arbor, Michigan.
